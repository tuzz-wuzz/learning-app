# US Kindergarten Curriculum

A comprehensive, standards-aligned curriculum for kindergarten students (ages 5–6).
Organized into 36 weeks across 7 subject areas.

## Subjects

| Subject | Weekly Time | Description |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| [Math](math.md) | 5 hrs | Numbers, counting, shapes, patterns, measurement |
| [English Language Arts](english.md) | 7 hrs | Phonics, reading, writing, speaking, listening |
| [Spanish](spanish.md) | 2.5 hrs | Introductory vocabulary, phrases, songs, culture |
| [Science](science.md) | 2.5 hrs | Life science, earth science, physical science |
| [History & Social Studies](history.md) | 2 hrs | Community, citizenship, holidays, geography |
| [Art](art.md) | 2 hrs | Drawing, painting, sculpture, art appreciation |
| [Music](music.md) | 2 hrs | Singing, rhythm, instruments, movement, listening |

## Curriculum Philosophy

- **Play-based learning**: Hands-on activities, games, and exploration
- **Developmentally appropriate**: Meets children where they are
- **Cross-curricular connections**: Subjects reinforce each other
- **Social-emotional growth**: Cooperation, self-regulation, and empathy woven throughout
- **Differentiated instruction**: Activities adaptable for varied skill levels

## Yearly Structure

| Quarter | Weeks | Theme |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Q1 | 1–9 | All About Me & My Community |
| Q2 | 10–18 | Exploring Our World |
| Q3 | 19–27 | How Things Work |
| Q4 | 28–36 | Growing & Changing |
