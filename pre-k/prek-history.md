# Pre-Kindergarten History & Social Studies Curriculum

Aligned with Head Start Early Learning Outcomes Framework (ELOF) for Social Emotional & Social Studies.

---

## Core Themes
- All About Me
- My Family & Home
- Making Friends & Using Kind Words
- My School Community
- Community Helpers

---

## Unit 1: All About Me (Weeks 1–6)

### Big Idea
I am special and unique.

### Learning Goals
- State first and last name
- Identify own age and gender
- Describe physical features (hair color, eye color)
- Share things they like and can do

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | Welcome to My Class | Self-portraits in mirrors, name songs |
| 2 | I Am ___ Years Old | Counting candles on a paper cake |
| 3 | My Body is Special | Measuring height with blocks, handprints |
| 4 | Things I Like to Do | "Show and Tell" favorite toys/activities |
| 5 | I Can Do It! | Celebrating "I can" moments (zipping, pouring) |
| 6 | My Name is Special | Decorating large letters of their name |

---

## Unit 2: My Family & Home (Weeks 7–12)

### Big Idea
Families are people who love and care for us.

### Learning Goals
- Identify family members (mom, dad, sister, brother, grandma, etc.)
- Understand that families can look different
- Share about special family traditions or meals

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 7 | Who is in My Family? | Drawing family pictures, family tree craft |
| 8 | We Live Together | Building "homes" with blocks or boxes |
| 9 | Helping at Home | Role-playing chores (sweeping, setting table) |
| 10 | Family Traditions | Sharing favorite family stories or songs |
| 11 | Pets are Family Too | Sharing about pets or pretend pets |
| 12 | Family Celebration | "Family Photo" wall creation |

---

## Unit 3: Friends & Feelings (Weeks 13–20)

### Big Idea
I can be a kind friend and talk about my feelings.

### Learning Goals
- Identify basic feelings (happy, sad, angry, scared)
- Practice taking turns and sharing
- Use "kind words" (please, thank you, I’m sorry)
- Understand the concept of a friend

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 13 | How Do I Feel? | Making "feeling faces" in mirrors, emotion masks |
| 14 | Happy & Sad | Sorting pictures of happy/sad situations |
| 15 | Angry & Scared | Learning "calm down" breaths (belly breaths) |
| 16 | What is a Friend? | "Friendship Soup" collaborative activity |
| 17 | Taking Turns | Games with a timer, "sharing" circle |
| 18 | Kind Words | "Bucket filling" with pompoms for kind acts |
| 19 | Helping Friends | Practicing asking "Are you okay?" |
| 20 | Friendship Party | Making friendship bracelets or cards |

---

## Unit 4: My School Community (Weeks 21–28)

### Big Idea
Our school is a place where we learn and play together.

### Learning Goals
- Recognize school staff (teacher, principal, cook, janitor)
- Follow school routines and rules
- Take care of school materials
- Identify different areas of the classroom

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 21 | Our Classroom "Homes" | Labeling centers (Blocks, Art, Library) |
| 22 | School Helpers | Touring the office, kitchen, and library |
| 23 | Caring for Our Toys | "Toy Wash" day, learning to put things away |
| 24 | Classroom Rules | Making a "Promise Tree" with handprints |
| 25 | The Playground | Learning safety rules and equipment names |
| 26 | School Bus Safety | "Driving" a cardboard bus, safety songs |
| 27 | Sharing Our Space | Collaborative mural for the hallway |
| 28 | School Spirit | Wearing school colors, school cheers |

---

## Unit 5: Community Helpers (Weeks 29–36)

### Big Idea
Many people in our community have jobs to help us.

### Learning Goals
- Identify helpers (firefighters, police, doctors, mail carriers)
- Understand what these helpers do and the tools they use
- Appreciate the different jobs people have

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 29 | Firefighters | Trying on "fire" hats, fire truck visit/video |
| 30 | Police Officers | Looking at badges, "Safety First" talk |
| 31 | Doctors & Nurses | Pretend clinic in the dramatic play center |
| 32 | Mail Carriers | Writing and "mailing" letters in the class mailbox |
| 33 | Dentists | Practicing brushing on large "teeth" models |
| 34 | Garbage & Recycling | Sorting trash vs. recycling, meeting the truck |
| 35 | Farmers & Chefs | "Market" play, where does food come from? |
| 36 | When I Grow Up... | Career dress-up day and sharing dreams |

---

## Materials
- Full-length and hand mirrors
- Emotion cards and posters
- Dramatic play costumes (doctor, chef, firefighter)
- Block people and diverse dolls
- Large paper for body tracings
- Classroom job chart
- Books about feelings and families
