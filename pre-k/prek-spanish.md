# Pre-Kindergarten Spanish Curriculum

An introductory world language program focusing on exposure and enthusiasm through play.

---

## Program Goals
- Become comfortable hearing and responding to a second language
- Learn high-frequency words (colors, numbers 1–10, family, animals)
- Participate in traditional Spanish songs and games
- Develop an early appreciation for diverse cultures

---

## Unit 1: Hello & Goodbye (Weeks 1–4)

### Vocabulary
- Hola (Hello)
- Adiós (Goodbye)
- ¿Cómo te llamas? (What is your name?)
- Me llamo ___ (My name is ___)
- Amigo/Amiga (Friend)

### Activities
- "Hola" greeting circle
- Puppet conversations
- Name songs in Spanish
- Passing a ball and saying "Hola, [Name]"

---

## Unit 2: Numbers 1–5 (Weeks 5–8)

### Vocabulary
- uno, dos, tres, cuatro, cinco

### Activities
- Counting fingers and toes
- Jumping while counting to cinco
- "Uno, dos, tres, amigos" song
- Counting blocks as we build a tower

---

## Unit 3: Primary Colors (Weeks 9–12)

### Vocabulary
- rojo (red)
- azul (blue)
- amarillo (yellow)
- verde (green)

### Activities
- Color scavenger hunt in the classroom
- Painting with one color at a time
- "Veo, veo" (I spy) with colors
- Sorting colorful toys into colored bins

---

## Unit 4: My Body (Mi Cuerpo) (Weeks 13–16)

### Vocabulary
- cabeza (head)
- manos (hands)
- pies (feet)
- ojos (eyes)
- nariz (nose)
- boca (mouth)

### Activities
- "Cabeza, Hombros..." song (Head, Shoulders...)
- Pointing to body parts on a doll
- Simon says: "Simón dice, toca la nariz"
- Body part sticker matching

---

## Unit 5: Animals (Los Animales) (Weeks 17–20)

### Vocabulary
- perro (dog)
- gato (cat)
- pez (fish)
- pájaro (bird)
- vaca (cow)

### Activities
- Making animal sounds in Spanish
- "El gato dice miau" matching game
- Animal mask parade
- Acting out animals (perro jumps, pez swims)

---

## Unit 6: Family (La Familia) (Weeks 21–24)

### Vocabulary
- mamá (mom)
- papá (dad)
- bebé (baby)
- abuela (grandma)
- abuelo (grandpa)

### Activities
- Drawing family members with labels
- "Te quiero, mamá/papá" cards
- Family finger puppets
- Sharing family photos and naming members

---

## Unit 7: Numbers 6–10 (Weeks 25–28)

### Vocabulary
- seis, siete, ocho, nueve, diez

### Activities
- Counting to diez during transitions
- Number hop (tape on the floor)
- "Diez Perritos" (Ten Little Dogs) song
- Counting snack pieces to diez

---

## Unit 8: Food (La Comida) (Weeks 29–32)

### Vocabulary
- manzana (apple)
- leche (milk)
- agua (water)
- pan (bread)
- Me gusta (I like)

### Activities
- Pretend "restaurante" play
- Sorting "fruta" (fruit)
- Taste testing (with permission) and saying "Me gusta"
- Food flashcard games

---

## Unit 9: Review & Fiesta (Weeks 33–36)

### Activities
- Reviewing favorite songs and games
- "Fiesta" with traditional music and snacks
- Sharing "What I learned in Spanish"
- End of year "Diplomas de Amigo"

---

## Traditional Songs & Rhymes
- **De Colores**: Song about colors and nature
- **Los Pollitos Dicen**: About baby chicks (pio, pio, pio)
- **Pin Pon**: About a puppet who washes his face
- **La Araña Pequeñita**: Itsy Bitsy Spider

---

## Materials
- Large, colorful flashcards
- Spanish children's music (e.g., José-Luis Orozco)
- Bilingual board books
- Puppets for role-playing
- Authentic artifacts (maracas, fabric, hats)
- Labels for classroom objects in Spanish
