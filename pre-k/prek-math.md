# Pre-Kindergarten Mathematics Curriculum

Aligned with Head Start Early Learning Outcomes Framework (ELOF) for Math.

---

## Strand 1: Number Sense & Counting (Weeks 1–15)

### Learning Goals
- Count to 20 by rote
- Demonstrate one-to-one correspondence for at least 10 objects
- Recognize and name numerals 0–10
- Understand "more," "less," and "the same"
- Identify the "last number counted" as the total quantity (cardinality)

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | Numbers 1–2 | Counting body parts (2 eyes, 1 nose) |
| 2 | Number 3 | "Three Little Pigs" sorting, 3-bears counting |
| 3 | Number 4 | Table setting (4 chairs, 4 plates) |
| 4 | Number 5 | High-five counting, finger songs |
| 5 | Review 1–5 | Number scavenger hunt, playdough numbers |
| 6 | More, Less, Same | Comparing piles of blocks, snack-time comparisons |
| 7 | Number 6 | Hexagon hunt (6 sides), counting buttons |
| 8 | Number 7 | Days of the week counting, rainbow colors |
| 9 | Number 8 | Spider leg counting, block towers |
| 10 | Numbers 9–10 | Full 10-frame exploration, "ten in the bed" song |
| 11 | Counting to 10 Review | Counting stairs, jumps, and claps |
| 12 | Numerals 0–5 | Matching numerals to dots on cards |
| 13 | Numerals 6–10 | Writing numerals in sand, finding numbers in the room |
| 14 | Introduction to 20 | Rote counting to 20 during transitions |
| 15 | Assessment | One-to-one correspondence check (up to 10) |

### Standards Covered
- P-MATH 1 – Knows number names and the count sequence
- P-MATH 2 – Recognizes the number of objects in a small set (subitizing)
- P-MATH 3 – Understands the relationship between numbers and quantities

## Strand 2: Operations & Algebraic Thinking (Weeks 16–22)

### Learning Goals
- Use concrete objects to explore "adding to" and "taking away"
- Recognize, duplicate, and extend simple patterns (AB, AABB)
- Sort objects by one attribute (color, shape, or size)

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 16 | Putting Together | Adding one more block to a tower |
| 17 | Taking Away | "Five Little Monkeys" song, eating snack pieces |
| 18 | Simple Patterns (AB) | Color patterns (red-blue-red-blue) |
| 19 | Patterns in Nature | Leaf-stone-leaf-stone, repeating movements |
| 20 | Sorting by Color | Rainbow sorting bins, laundry sort |
| 21 | Sorting by Shape | Circle vs. Square baskets |
| 22 | Sorting by Size | Small, medium, and large bear sorting |

### Standards Covered
- P-MATH 6 – Understands addition as adding to and subtraction as taking away
- P-MATH 7 – Recognizes and creates patterns
- P-MATH 8 – Sorts and classifies objects by one or more attributes

---

## Strand 3: Measurement & Data (Weeks 23–27)

### Learning Goals
- Compare objects using measurement vocabulary (big/small, long/short, heavy/light)
- Use non-standard units to measure (hands, blocks, links)
- Participate in simple data collection (graphing favorites)

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 23 | Big & Small | Sorting toys by size, "Goldilocks" comparison |
| 24 | Long & Short | Comparing pieces of yarn, stick measurements |
| 25 | Heavy & Light | Holding objects, using a simple balance scale |
| 26 | Measuring with Blocks | "How many blocks long is your foot?" |
| 27 | Favorite Color Graph | Placing a sticker on a class chart |

### Standards Covered
- P-MATH 9 – Measures objects by using non-standard units
- P-MATH 10 – Identifies and compares measurable attributes of objects

---

## Strand 4: Geometry & Spatial Sense (Weeks 28–36)

### Learning Goals
- Identify basic 2D shapes (circle, square, triangle, rectangle)
- Use positional words (on, under, beside, in front of, behind)
- Compose simple shapes using smaller shapes (tangrams)

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

| Week | Topic | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| 28 | Circle & Square | Shape hunt, drawing in shaving cream |
| 29 | Triangle & Rectangle | Sorting snacks by shape, building with shapes |
| 30 | Shape Art | Making a house out of a square and triangle |
| 31 | Up & Down | Following directions on the playground |
| 32 | In, On, Under | "Where is the teddy bear?" game |
| 33 | Beside & Behind | Obstacle courses, positional hide-and-seek |
| 34 | 3D Shapes Intro | Rolling balls (spheres) vs. stacking blocks (cubes) |
| 35 | Building with Shapes | Using blocks to create "cities" |
| 36 | Review & Celebration | Math games day, shape pizza party |

### Standards Covered
- P-MATH 11 – Identifies, describes, and compares shapes
- P-MATH 12 – Understands directionality, order, and position of objects

---

## Materials
- Counting bears and link chains
- Large wooden and soft blocks
- Attribute buttons for sorting
- Balance scale (non-standard)
- Shape sorters and puzzles
- Number lines (0–10) with pictures
- 10-frames (physical mats)
