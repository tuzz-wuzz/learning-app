# Kindergarten Spanish Curriculum

An introductory world language program using immersive, play-based methods (ACTFL-aligned).

---

## Program Goals
- Develop basic listening comprehension and oral communication
- Build foundational vocabulary (200+ words by year end)
- Foster cultural awareness and appreciation of Spanish-speaking communities
- Encourage enthusiasm for language learning

---

## Unit 1: Greetings & Introductions (Weeks 1–4)

### Vocabulary & Phrases
- Hola, adiós, buenos días, buenas tardes, buenas noches
- ¿Cómo te llamas? Me llamo ___
- ¿Cómo estás? Bien, mal, así así
- Por favor, gracias, de nada

### Activities
- Greeting songs and chants
- Puppet introductions
- Partner greeting practice
- "Buenos días" morning routine

---

## Unit 2: Numbers 1–10 (Weeks 5–7)

### Vocabulary
- uno, dos, tres, cuatro, cinco, seis, siete, ocho, nueve, diez

### Activities
- Counting songs ("Uno, dos, tres" rhymes)
- Number hopscotch (la rayuela)
- Dice games with Spanish counting
- Counting objects in the classroom

---

## Unit 3: Colors (Weeks 8–10)

### Vocabulary
- rojo, azul, amarillo, verde, anaranjado, morado, rosado, negro, blanco, marrón/café

### Activities
- Color scavenger hunts
- "¿De qué color es?" guessing game
- Painting while naming colors in Spanish
- Color song ("De colores")

---

## Unit 4: Family (La Familia) (Weeks 11–13)

### Vocabulary
- mamá, papá, hermano, hermana, abuela, abuelo, bebé, familia
- Te quiero (I love you)

### Activities
- Family drawings with Spanish labels
- Family tree craft
- Read-aloud: bilingual family books
- Role-play with family vocabulary

---

## Unit 5: Body Parts (El Cuerpo) (Weeks 14–16)

### Vocabulary
- cabeza, hombros, rodillas, pies, ojos, orejas, boca, nariz, manos, dedos, brazos, piernas

### Activities
- "Cabeza, hombros, rodillas, pies" song and dance
- Body part bingo
- Drawing and labeling a person
- Simon says: "Simón dice, toca la nariz"

---

## Unit 6: Animals (Los Animales) (Weeks 17–20)

### Vocabulary
- perro, gato, pez, pájaro, vaca, caballo, cerdo, pollo, rana, mariposa
- Animal sounds in Spanish (guau guau, miau, muuu, quiquiriquí)

### Activities
- Animal flashcard games
- "¿Qué animal es?" guessing game
- Animal masks and role play
- Read-aloud: *De la cabeza a los pies* (From Head to Toe)

---

## Unit 7: Food (La Comida) (Weeks 21–24)

### Vocabulary
- manzana, plátano/banana, uva, naranja, leche, agua, pan, queso, arroz, pollo
- Tengo hambre (I'm hungry), Tengo sed (I'm thirsty)
- Me gusta / No me gusta (I like / I don't like)

### Activities
- Food sorting games
- Pretend restaurant role-play
- Taste-testing fruits with Spanish names
- "Me gusta" survey and graphing

---

## Unit 8: Clothing (La Ropa) (Weeks 25–27)

### Vocabulary
- camisa, pantalones, zapatos, calcetines, vestido, chaqueta, sombrero, falda

### Activities
- Paper doll dressing with Spanish labels
- "¿Qué llevas?" (What are you wearing?) partner practice
- Weather-based clothing matching
- Fashion show with narration in Spanish

---

## Unit 9: Weather & Seasons (El Tiempo y Las Estaciones) (Weeks 28–30)

### Vocabulary
- sol, lluvia, nieve, viento, nube, calor, frío
- primavera, verano, otoño, invierno
- ¿Qué tiempo hace? Hace sol, Hace frío, Llueve, Nieva

### Activities
- Daily weather report in Spanish
- Season sorting activity
- Weather wheel craft
- Songs: "Lluvia, lluvia, vete ya" (Rain, Rain, Go Away)

---

## Unit 10: My School & Classroom (Mi Escuela) (Weeks 31–33)

### Vocabulary
- escuela, clase, maestro/maestra, libro, lápiz, papel, tijeras, pegamento, mesa, silla, mochila
- Puedo ir al baño? (May I go to the bathroom?)

### Activities
- Classroom labeling in Spanish
- School supply scavenger hunt
- Classroom commands practice (siéntate, levántate, escucha)
- Read-aloud: bilingual school-themed books

---

## Unit 11: Days of the Week & Months (Weeks 34–35)

### Vocabulary
- lunes, martes, miércoles, jueves, viernes, sábado, domingo
- enero, febrero, marzo, abril, mayo, junio, julio, agosto, septiembre, octubre, noviembre, diciembre
- ¿Qué día es hoy? Hoy es ___

### Activities
- Calendar routine in Spanish
- Days of the week song ("La canción de los días")
- Birthday month graphing in Spanish

---

## Unit 12: Review & Cultural Celebration (Week 36)

### Activities
- Vocabulary review games and stations
- Cultural celebration: music, food, and traditions from Spanish-speaking countries
- Student presentations: "Me llamo ___, me gusta ___"
- Fiesta de fin de año (end-of-year party)

---

## Cultural Connections (Integrated Throughout)

| Event | Approximate Timing | Activities |
|-------|-------------------|------------|
| Día de los Muertos | November | Sugar skull decorating, marigold crafts, altar discussion |
| Las Posadas | December | Piñata making, holiday songs |
| Día de los Reyes | January | Crown crafts, Rosca de Reyes |
| Carnaval | February/March | Masks, music, dance |
| Cinco de Mayo | May | Cultural exploration, crafts, music |

---

## Teaching Strategies
- **Total Physical Response (TPR)**: Pair vocabulary with actions and movement
- **Music & song**: Learn through repetition in catchy songs
- **Visual supports**: Flashcards, realia (real objects), picture dictionaries
- **Repetition & routine**: Daily Spanish greeting, calendar, weather
- **Comprehensible input**: Use simple Spanish with gestures and visuals
- **Cultural immersion**: Stories, music, and art from Spanish-speaking countries

---

## Materials
- Flashcard sets for each unit
- Bilingual picture books
- Spanish music and song recordings
- Puppets for conversation practice
- Cultural artifacts and decorations
